**The privacy-first PDF toolkit that runs entirely in your browser.**

PDFYantra is a free, simple tool to help you manage your PDF documents without worrying about privacy. Unlike other online tools, PDFYantra **does not upload your files to any server**. Everything happens right on your device, ensuring your sensitive documents stay safe.

## ğŸŒŸ Features

*   **Organize & Merge:** Drag and drop multiple PDF files. Rearrange pages, rotate them, delete the ones you don't need, and combine everything into a single new document.
*   **Split Documents:** Extract specific pages or split a large document into smaller parts (like chapters) automatically.
*   **Convert Formats:**
    *   Turn PDF pages into images (JPG, PNG) or plain text.
    *   Create PDFs from images, Word docs, Excel sheets, or text files.
*   **Compress:** Reduce the file size of your PDFs to make them easier to share via email.
*   **Visual Editor:** See exactly what you are doing with high-quality thumbnail previews of every page.
*   **Dark Mode:** A beautiful interface that looks great day or night.

## ğŸ“– How to Use

1.  **Open the App:** Launch PDFYantra in your web browser.
